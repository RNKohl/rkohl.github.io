A Biography of Ryan Kohl

Early Life

Ryan Kohl was born in Connecticut in the mid 1990s. He is the first of two children. As a child Ryan learned to love the sea from his father, who took him fishing and lobstering every week during the summer months. A lifelong lover of music, Ryan began to play the Piano at age 5 and the viola at age 8. He would continue to play both throughout high school.

Education

From an early age, Ryan was a voracious reader, which fostered a life long love of learning. His favorite genre is historical non-fiction, and he greatly enjoys learning about the history of regions across the globe. As a high schooler, Ryan was president of his school's Model United Nations Club, The Principal violist of the local youth symphony, and a consistent honor roll student. He graduated high school in 2013 and went on to begin his undergraduate education at Tufts University the following fall. As an undergrad Ryan grew to enjoy his math courses so much that he changed his major from Chemistry to Applied Mathematics.
